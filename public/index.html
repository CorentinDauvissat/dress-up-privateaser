Welcome to this page !

Privateaser is a website to easily book a bar, a restaurant or a room for you and your friends or your colleagues.
Here, you can have the price of your booking, according to the features fixed by the bar :

  • The price per hour
  • The price per person
  • The time in hour
  • The number of persons

You can also choose a deductible option in case of an accident/theft
